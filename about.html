About us

We are an award winning Agency

take a look at our story

Reisz Travels is dedicated to unsurpassed customer service. Our travel experts provide you exclusive experience around the world. We craft and create ordinary to extraordinary holidays for you to discover the most sought-after and glamorous destinations. We also stayed ahead of the curve by continually evolving its technology to meet the ever changing demands of the rapidly developing global travel market.

Our commitment and customer-centricity allows us to better understand and fulfill customers’ diverse needs and wants, and deliver consistently. You may see the same price with one of those corporate booking sites, but you get the best customer service and personal attention with us!

Send us a little information about your next trip and one of our agents who specialize in your destination will reach out to you within 24 hours. Also, feel free to reach out to us via telephone or social media.

We entirely consist of professionals and experienced team to guide every client, depending on their necessity within the stipulated time period. We follow an arranged process of solutions to our customers to award them with honest, reliable and accurate guidance.

Our reach is not limited to one county but encompasses many including Antarctica, Australia, Bali, Canada, China, Dubai, Fiji, France, Greece, Hong Kong & Macau, Italy, Jordan, Kenya, Malaysia, Maldives, Mauritius, New Zealand, Scandinavia, Singapore, South Africa, Spain, Sri Lanka, Switzerland, Thailand, Turkey, United Kingdom, USA, Vietnam and counting. We not only provide guidance for holiday packages but also Travel Insurance, Visa assistance and also Cruise option is available. Reisz Travels provides a complete package, where you can be absolutely free from stress and tension.

Our dedication to outstanding professionalism has won the attention of a number of the leading forces and this has allowed us to achieve a new level of mutual trust and understanding with our clients. We make sure to grant them with timely solutions and absolute transparency in every service offered. Our dedication and commitments we have made our reputation of being a reliable and most assured company in the realm.

Andaman
  • 1 person
  • 5 nights
  • 6 days
  • 3 star hotel

Andaman and nicobar are one of the most amazing travel destinations of India. The islands are popular for sparkling blue water and white sandy beaches. Here are the best places to visit in Andaman and Nicobar Islands - Bharatpur beach, cellular jail, limestone caves, aquarium and marine park, radhanagar beach, neil islands,guitar island beach, port blair, rangat, mayabunder, nlmahatma gandhi marine national park, mount harriet national park, the great nicobar biosphere reserve, ray hill, kaala pathar, etc. You will never be short of doing things to do in Andaman. If you want more information, our travel experts are ultimate guide to plan your trip.

DAY 01 – Arrive at Port Blair

Arrive at Port Blair and you will be taken to your where you relax and rest and after lunch ( on your own) in the evening you will be taken to Cellular Jail (closed on Mondays) a place of pilgrimage for all freedom loving people to view the history of the heroic freedom struggle which is brought alive in a moving Light and Sound Show .Evening is free for leisure activities. Overnight at the hotel.

DAY 02 – Port Blair to Havelock

After breakfast, you will proceed from Port Blair to Havelock by Makruzz ferry. On arrival proceed to your hotel. In the afternoon proceed to world famous Radha Nagar Beach (Beach No 7) rated as the 'Best Beach in Asia' by Time Magazine. Watch a mesmerizing sunset on Beach no.7 before returning back to your hotel to relax and unwind. Overnight at the hotel.

DAY 03 - Havelock - Port Blair

After breakfast you will check-out from your hotel and proceed to jetty for your return ferry to Port Blair. On arrival get a speedy transfer to Hotel. In the evening Visit Corbyns Cove Beach one of the most picturesque, coconut palm fringed beach, 7 kms away from Port Blair town. Return back to hotel. Overnight at the hotel in Port Blair.

DAY 04 – Port Blair

After breakfast proceed to Ross Island, the erstwhile capital of Port Blair during the British regime. The island presently houses the ruins of old buildings like Chief Commissioner's House, Government House, Church, Bakery, Press, Swimming Pool, Cemetery, etc. and all in dilapidated condition. This island is an ideal destination for nature walk amidst sylvan surroundings with deer, peacocks, exotic birds, etc. Return to hotel for lunch. Post Lunch proceed to ChidiyaTapu (also known as Sunset Point) is one of the best places to see the sunset in Port Blair, The beach is located on the furthest Southern tip of South Andaman Island 25 kms South of Port Blair and takes about 45 minutes to get there by car. Overnight at the hotel.

DAY 05 - Port Blair - Sightseeing

The day's program begins with the sightseeing tour in and around the city like Naval Marine Museum (Samudrika) - gives a good retrospect of the history, shells, Corals marine life, geography and archaeology of these islands, Mini zoo - houses some of the rare species of endemic birds and animals found in these islands, Forest museum - offers an insight into forest activities through scale models and displays decorative pieces made of famous woods, Anthropological Museum - displays tribal essential and artefacts. After lunch, proceed for Fisheries Museum (Aquarium) - exhibits species of marine life peculiar to the islands, and then to the Cellular Jail, a place of pilgrimage for all freedom loving people. Evening is free for Shopping.

DAY 06 - Depart Port Blair

After breakfast transfer to Port Blair airport in time to board your flight. Depart Port Blair with amzing memories of the bwitch islands.

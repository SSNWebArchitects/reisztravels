China

China boasts a large number of magnificent tour destinations including subtle water towns, sites and relics, astonishing natural wonders, impressive imperial palaces, marvellous cultural heritage and expanded folk customs. With Reisz Travels you can visit all these highlights throughout the country. Our travel packages cover all top attractions such as the well-known Great Wall of China, astonishing terracotta army, Forbidden City, Yangtze River, potala palace, Li River, Silk Road, summer palace, temple of heaven etc. We always endeavour to show visitors enjoyable panoramic view of china tourism with endless green mountains. If you are planning for holiday, we are the travel experts at Reisz Travels.

DAY 01 – ARRIVE BEIJING

On arrival in Beijing, you will be greeted by the Glorius Journey Tour Manager/local representative outside the baggage hall area at the airport. Proceed to the hotel and Check in. In the evening enjoy the famous Golden Mask Dynasty show - an awe inspiring show with grand sets, colorful costumes and spectacular performances. Tonight enjoy a delicious Indian dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Jiangxi Grand Hotel or similar in Beijing

DAY 02 – Great Wall of China - Aqua Theatre - the "Bird's Nest" stadium

After breakfast, proceed on a guided tour of the renowned Great Wall of China, stretching to a length of around 5500 miles across China, we visit the Juyongguan pass section of the wall. Experience local Chinese lunch. Get a chance to see the Olympics Venue of 2008 with its Aqua Theatre, the "Bird's Nest" stadium and the dragon shaped Olympic Office. You will also visit a local Chinese antique store. Tonight enjoy a delicious Indian dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Jiangxi Grand Hotel or similar in Beijing

DAY 03 – Tian'anmen Square, Forbidden City

Enjoy the sights and sounds of Beijing on your guided city tour. Visit Tian'anmen Square, Forbidden City. Onto Xian the ancient city of Chang'an! Visit the famous Wild Goose Pagoda with its magical fountains. after breakfast check out and proceed on an exciting city tour of Beijing. Visit Tian'anmen Square, one of the largest squares in the world, named after the Tiananmen Gate (Gate of Heavenly Peace) located to its North, separating it from the Forbidden City. It has great cultural significance as it was the site of several important events in Chinese history. Later see the Forbidden City which was the Chinese imperial palace from the Ming Dynasty to the end of the Qing Dynasty. For almost 500 years, it served as the home of emperors and their households, as well as the ceremonial and political center of Chinese government. Enjoy your packed Indian lunch. Then get on board the high speed train to Xian. Arrive and proceed to the Wild Goose Pagoda, where you will also see the magical colorful and musical fountains which will leave you in awe. Tonight enjoy a delicious Indian dinner at a local restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Jianguo or similar in Xian

DAY 04 – Xian Terra - Cotta warriors

Visit the world famous Xian Terra-Cotta warriors and enjoy a short 360-degree film! Onto Shanghai - the river city after breakfast, visit the famous Terra Cotta warriors; where you can see life-sized colorful Terra Cotta warriors and horses arranged in battle formation. Also enjoy a 360 degree film presentation about the terracotta army; its history and formation. Enjoy your Indian lunch. Later proceed to the airport to board the flight to Shanghai. Arrive and proceed to the financial capital to China Shanghai. This evening you have the chance to enjoy the famous Acrobatic show where top artists display their prowess at trick cycling, spinning, juggling and Chinese dancing. Proceed for a delicious dinner at a local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Days Hotel Shanghai Hongkou Fortune or similar in Shanghai

DAY 05 - Observatory Deck of PearlTower

Enjoy a guided city tour of Shanghai followed by TV visit to observatory deck of PearlTower to get the panoramic view of the city. Get on board the Huangpu River Cruise. Today, after an American breakfast, join us on an exciting city tour of Shanghai. Visit Yuyuvan Gardens, the world renowned Shanghai Museum with a collection of 120,000 precious works of art, followed by a visit to the Bund Area. Enjoy your Indian Lunch. Enjoy some free time for bargain shopping at the famous Nanjing Road. In the evening, get ready to see splendid views of the city from the observatory deck of the oriental pearl TV Tower. Later get on board the Huangpu River Cruise and enjoy the glimpses of the city skyline. Proceed for a delicious dinner at a local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Days Hotel Shanghai Hongkou Fortune or similar in Shanghai

DAY 06 - Ancient Watertown - Zhujiajiao

after breakfast, proceed for a day trip to visit the ancient watertown - Zhujiajiao. This town is well-known throughout the country, with a history of more than 1700 years. Covering an area of 47 square kilometers, the little fan-shaped town glimmers like a bright pearl in the landscape of lakes and mountains. See the nique old bridges across bubbling streams, small rivers shaded by willow trees, and houses with courtyards. Bridges here are distinctive and old, built during Ming and Qing Dynasties. The old town is thoroughly connected by 36 delicate spans in different shapes and styles, from wooden to stone to marble. While exploring this town enjoy local lunch. Later drive back to Shanghai. In the evening proceed for a delicious dinner at a local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Days Hotel Shanghai Hongkou Fortune or similar in Shanghai

DAY 07 – Fly back to home

Enjoy breakfast at your hotel. Later today, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your tour with Reisz Travels.

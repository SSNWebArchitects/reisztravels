DAY 01 – Arrive at Nice

Arrive at Nice International Airport. Meet & Greet at Airport. Private Transfer to Hotel. In the evening enjoy, Monaco and Monte Carlo by Night Tour.  Feel the magic of Monaco and Monte-Carlo by night where the rich and the famous can be seen gambling and dining in the most prestigious restaurants. We begin the tour with a drive on the spectacular 'MoyenneCorniche' road to Monaco. First discover the quiet atmosphere of the Prince's Palace area. Then experience the glamorous extravagance of Monte-Carlo by night. The Casino, the 'Hotel de Paris' & the Hermitage form an incomparable ensemble. You will enjoy free time to stroll in the streets of Monte-Carlo, have a drink or dine in one of the famous restaurants. And why not trying your luck in the Casino. You can take advantage of the many opportunities to savour the splendid atmosphere of Monaco's night life. This night of magic will end up by a return to Nice via the 'BasseCorniche' road where more illuminated seaside will pass before your eyes. Overnight at Hotel

DAY 02 – Nice & Surrounding Areas

Breakfast will serve in Hotel. After that enjoy Full day tour of the Cote d'Azur takes us to Eze, Monaco & Monte Carlo, St Paul de Vence, Antibes and Cannes. Upon leaving Nice, we drive up to Mont Alban, with its splendid views over Nice and Villefranche. Then on to Eze, a typical French medieval village, famous for perfume making. You will have the chance to visit one of the perfume factories to see how the local scents are made. From there, we travel the Principality of Monaco to see Old Monaco, the Cathedral, changing of the guards at noon and the famous Formula 1 Grand Prix Circuit. Then to St Paul de Vence, the jewel of Provence, where you will appreciate the artistic atmosphere. From scenic St Paul de Vence, we drive to Antibes, to see the old town, and marvel at the enormous yachts moored at 'Billionaires' Quay'. Our final visit of the day is to Cannes, where we visit the 'Croisette' to see the prints of some of the famous actors who have come to Cannes for the film festival. We will arrive back in Nice at around 17.30. Overnight at Hotel

DAY 03 - Nice - Paris

Check Out from Nice Hotel. Private transfers from Nice Hotel to Airport to board flight to Paris. Arrive at Paris International Airport. Meet & Greet at Airport. Private Transfer to Hotel. In the evening enjoy Cruise and Illuminations tour of Paris. This is combined tour with a cruise on the River Seine followed by a drive through Paris by night. You will start your evening with a cruise on the River Seine to see the main monuments. This will be followed by a magical and magnificent drive through Paris by night with its beautiful illuminated buildings where you will be delighted by the sight of the champs-Elysees, the Arc of Triumph, the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Pyramid and many more. Overnight at Hotel.

DAY 04 – Disneyland Tour

Breakfast will serve in Hotel. After that enjoy Full day tour of Disneyland Resort Paris Entrance to Two Parks or One park. You can spend the day at the two parks or one park - Disneyland and Disney Studio at the fantastic world of Disneyland Resort Paris. This is a one day pass to Disneyland Resort Paris that gives a full day in the two parks. This includes return transfers on board a luxury motor coach from the centre of Paris to Disneyland Paris and admission ticket for Walt Disney Studios Park and the Disneyland Park. Once you step through the Walt Disney Studios Gates, let your imagination soar and immerse yourself in the never-ending, always fascinating world of movies and television. When you enter the Disneyland Theme Parks, an enchanting kingdom filled with fun, fantasy and discovery will form before your eyes, a world of make-believe characters, shows and attractions. Whichever park you visit, you will discover the most magical place on earth inspired by the most magical dream of all: yours. Overnight at Hotel

DAY 05 - Paris Tour – Either Paris Tour or Versailles Tour or Guided Tour of Louvre Museum

Breakfast will serve in Hotel. After that enjoy 6 hours tour of Paris Essentials Tour, Cruise and Lunch at Eiffel Tower Paris to view its main monuments including a one-hour cruise on the Seine River and lunch on the 1st floor of the Eiffel Tower A strong characteristic of the French people is the respect they have for their history and the restoration of their national monuments. The result of this preservation of the past has greatly contributed to the beauty of Paris today. This tour includes the view from coach of the main attractions of Paris such as Place de la Concorde, the Avenue des Champs Elysees, Arc de Triomphe and much more. Then you will enjoy a one hour cruise on the River Seine. Once back to the embankment you will be taken to the 1st floor at the Eiffel Tower for a lunch at the new restaurant Le 58eme. From there you will admire the superb panoramic view of Paris. The tour will end at the Eiffel Tower so you can enjoy this famous tourist area. In case of sudden closure of Eiffel Tower due to strike etc The meal at 58 Tour Eiffel restaurants will be replaced by restaurant ''Cafe Seine'' or similar

OR

Full Day at Versailles by Minibus or by Private Car (8 hours or 5 hours)

Paris, France

A great opportunity to spend a day visiting Versailles Palace to discover not only the castle but the gardens and the trianons with a lunch in the park Guided visit of the State Apartments (Hall of Mirrors, King's and Queen's Bedroom) and the park of Versailles. After the visit, enjoy lunch (Drink included) in the restaurant "La Petite Venise" near the Grand Canal inside the park of Versailles Castle. In the afternoon guided visit of the Petit and the Grand Trianons followed by Queen Marie-Antoinette's Hamlet. Free time to visit the gardens

OR

Guided Tour of the Louvre (2 hours 30 minutes)

Paris, France

Enjoy a guided tour of the Louvre Museum with the visit of the main works the Venus de Milo, the Winged Victory of Samothrace and the Mona Lisa. You will take to a guided tour of the Louvre Museum to admire its 3 main works: the Venus de Milo, the Winged Victory of Samothrace and the Mona Lisa, painted by Leonardo da Vinci at the beginning of the 16th century. You will then be free to spend a little more time in the Louvre to visit other sections of the largest museum in the world. Overnight at Hotel

DAY 06 - Return to India

Breakfast will serve in Hotel. Check out from Hotel & transfer to Airport to board your return flight to India.

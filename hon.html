Hong Kong & Macau
  • 1 person
  • 5 nights
  • 6 days
  • 3 star hotel

The exceptional blend of British colonial and Portuguese influence makes one charming vacation. Experience the beautiful cities with the exciting places to visit. Discover the cathedrals and eminent forts at Macau and enjoy the stunning Hong Kong. Delight the fabulous views of Hong Kong and visit the legendary Disney Land and Madam Tussauds. We offer you astonishing holiday package to Hong Kong and Macau and we will make sure that you remember your trip forever.

DAY 01 – Hong Kong and Macau

Arrive into Hong Kong and proceed to Macau. Welcome to Hong Kong, on arrival proceed to Skypier and board the ferry to Macau, you will be greeted by your friendly Reisz Travels Tour Manager / local representative. Proceed to your hotel, check in and relax. In the evening enjoy a delicious dinner at local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Venetian Macau or similar in Macau

DAY 02 – Historic city of Macau - A-Ma temple

Enjoy city tour of the historic city of Macau visiting A-Ma temple, ruins of St. Paul's church, Golden Lotus Square, Fisherman Wharf, Photo Stop at Macau Tower. Today after an American breakfast at the hotel, proceed for an exciting city tour of Macau. Visit A-Ma temple Ruins of St. Paul's church, Golden Lotus Square, Fisherman Wharf and then photo stop at Macau Tower. In the evening, you can also try your luck at the numerous Casinos (on your own). Tonight enjoy a hot sumptuous dinner at local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Venetian Macau or similar in Macau

DAY 03 – Hong Kong - Aberdeen Harbour, Repulse Bay Beach

Proceed to Hong Kong. Enjoy city tour of Hong Kong. Visit Aberdeen Harbour, Repulse Bay Beach, One way Peak Tram and the famous Victoria Peak Today morning after an American breakfast, proceed to Hong Kong by ferry. On arrival get set for a city tour of this quaint city - get ready to catch a glimpse of the life-style of Hong Kong's remaining fisher folk as you visit the Aberdeen Harbour. Also visit the most famous beach in Hong Kong - Repulse Bay Beach. Post lunch proceed to the top attraction spot of Hong Kong - Victoria Peak. Enjoy one way Peak tram ride. View the Victoria Peak which is a 1503 foot above the sea level gives a breathtaking panoramic view of the city. Then visit Jewellery Factory and Canton Road. Tonight enjoy a hot sumptuous dinner at local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Regal Oriental or similar in Hong Kong.

DAY 04 – Macau - Ocean Park, the Casino Capital of Asia!

Morning visit Ocean Park, afternoon proceed to Macau - the Casino Capital of Asia! Today after an American breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Ocean Park Hong Kong. The first Asian winner of the biannual Applause Award, the most prestigious award in the amusement and theme park industry. The park is divided into 2 areas and has more than 70 attractions: The Waterfront and The Summit areas are connected by the Cable Car and Ocean Express funicular train. Meet more than 100 sharks and rays in one of the largest shark exhibits in Asia, Shark Mystique. Pay a visit to Polar Adventure, home of penguins and other polar animals. Then scream like crazy on Thrill Mountain's wild rides! Amazing Asian Animals is home to many precious animals, including giant pandas, red pandas and golden monkeys. Take an awe-inspiring tour through a world of fish at the Aqua City, and discover the fun tastes of yesteryears in Old Hong Kong! For lunch inside Ocean Park, Lunch Coupon will be provided by our Tour Manager. In the evening get ready to enjoy a delicious dinner at local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Regal Oriental or similar in Hong Kong

DAY 05 - Disneyland - Hong Kong's

Day free or opt for full day visit to Disneyland - Hong Kong's most famous attraction. Today after an American breakfast at the hotel, day is free to explore the city on your own or you can opt for a full day visit to Hong Kong Disneyland. Get ready for some fun, adventure and entertainment. Enjoy the 'Lion King' show, see the delightful Mickeys Philar magic, see the outer-galaxy at the 'Space Mountain'. Post lunch; prepare to be dazzled by the spectacular Disney Parade where all your loved Disney characters come to life. Begin new adventures in the western gold rush township known as Grizzly Gulch, exclusive to Hong Kong Disneyland! Explore the goldmines and the entire land of Grizzly Gulch on a high-speed, multi-directional terrain-style coaster Big Grizzly Mountain "Runaway Mine Cars", and turn to the town's active geyser bed Geyser Gulch for some great water play escapades. Come unearth your adventure in the great Wild West! If you still haven't had your fill of fun, head for playtime in Toy Story Land. Andy's speediest toy car RC Racer will take you on a 27 meter-high U-shaped roller-coaster ride that will get your adrenaline pumping. Then your mission includes falling from 25 meters high in the Toy Story Parachute Drop and chasing your tail in the Slinky Dog Spin. End the day with the dazzling firework show (subject to weather conditions). After a fun filled day at the Hong Kong Theme Park, proceed to enjoy a delicious dinner at local Indian restaurant. Overnight at Hotel Regal Oriental or similar in Hong Kong

DAY 06 - Fly back home

Today after an American breakfast at your hotel, proceed to the airport as this fantastic tour comes to an end. Return home with wonderful memories of your tour with Reisz Travels.

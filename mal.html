Malaysia
  • 1 person
  • 6 nights
  • 7 days
  • 3 star hotel

Malaysia is a blend of different customs, cultures, religions and cuisines. If countries were awarded for multiplicity, Malaysia would top foremost place. From mountains to large islands, tropical rainforest to fertile highlands, every bit is diverse. Malaysia is divided into two main landmasses. East Malaysia is across the South China Sea and west Malaysia occupies the southern half of a peninsula shared with Thailand. Here, the overview of the best places in Malaysia – Kota Bharu, Melaka, Cameron Highlands, Kota Kinabalu, Kuching, Penang, Kuala Lumpur, Taman Negara, Langkawi, Gunung Mulu National Park.

DAY 01 – ARRIVE AT LANGKAWI

On arrival after you have collected your luggage you will meet and greet by our local representative and get transfers from Langkawi airport to Hotel on Seat in Coach Basis. Normal Check in time at hotel 14:00 Hrs. evening is free for own activities.Overnight Stay at your Choice of Hotel

DAY 02 – HALF DAY LANGKAWI ISLAND TOUR

After Breakfast enjoy Half day at Langkawi Island Tour on Seat in Coach Basis.An introductory tour of Pulau Langkawi Visit Atma Alam Batik Art Village, which showcases this native art and its applications from articles of clothing to interior decorations At Mahsuri's Tomb, learn about the legend surrounding this wronged Princess and her curse of seven generations of ill luck for the inhabitants of Langkawi. On the northern shore of the island visit the unusual Black Sand Beach, so stained as a result of its mineral content such as iron oxide. The island's modest capital is Kuah Town, the location of Eagle Square, a large statue depicting an eagle about to take flight. The island received its name from the colloquial Malay word for the reddish-brown eagles that can often be seen soaring overhead in the countryside. Take advantage of Langkawi duty-free status for cut-price goods at Kuah's several outlets. Afternoon is free for own activities. Overnight Stay at your Choice of Hotel

DAY 03 – DAY AT LEISURE

Breakfast will serve at the hotel. Entire day is free for own activities. Overnight Stay at your Choice of Hotel

DAY 04 – DEPART TO KUALA LUMPUR

Check out Langkawi Hotel, transfer to Airport to board flight to Kuala Lumpur, on arrival after you have collected your luggage you will meet and greet by our representative and then get transfer from Kuala Lumpur Hotel on Seat in Coach Basis. Normal Check in time at hotel 14:00 Hrs.Evening is free for own activities. Overnight Stay at your Choice of Hotel

DAY 05 - SUNWAY LAGOON THEME PARK TOUR

Breakfast will serve at the hotel.After Breakfast enjoy full day tour to Sunway lagoon Theme Park with 5 park tickets. There is Water Park, Amusement park, Wild Life Park, Extreme Park & Scream Park. Later in late afternoon transfer to Kuala Lumpur Hotel on Seat in Coach Basis. Overnight at Kuala Lumpur Hotel

DAY 06 - HALF DAY KUALA LUMPUR CITY TOUR

Breakfast will serve at the hotel.Kuala Lumpur City tour will cover both beauty and charm of the old and new Kuala Lumpur - Garden City of Lights. See the contrast of the magnificent skyscrapers and the buildings of the colonial days. Highlights of the tour include; Petronas Twin Towers (photo stop), OE jewellery showroom, King's Palace (photo stop), National Monument, National Museum (excludes entrance fee), National Mosque (photo stop) and the Sultan Abdul Samad Building. Afternoon is free for own activities. Overnight Stay at your Choice of Hotel

DAY 07 – DEPART FROM KUALA LUMPUR

After breakfast in the Hotel, you will check-out from hotel & proceed towards airport to board Flight back to home with happy memories.

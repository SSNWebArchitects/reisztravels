Mauritius
  • 1 person
  • 5 nights
  • 6 days
  • 3 star hotel

At the idea of Mauritius, the only pictures that juggle up are those of its azure lagoons, palm trees and sandy beaches. It has tons of breath-taking views, blossoming forests and historic sites that are to be seen. Here is the quick guide about the best places in Mauritius – Le Morne, Black River Gorges national Park, Chamarel, La Vanille Crocodile Park, Pamplemousses Botanical Garden, Chateau de Labourdonnais, Ile Aux Cerfs, La roche qui pleure, Grand Bassin, Champ de Mars in Port Louis. If you are planning on visiting Mauritius, we are your ultimate travel guide.

DAY 01 – Arrive Mauritius

Upon arrival to Mauritius International Airport, you will be met by local representative who will assist you with the transfer to the hotel in Mauritius where you check-in. Rest of the day is at leisure to explore this beautiful city on your own. Mauritius, a sparkling crystal in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, will fascinate you. The contrast of colours, cultures and tastes makes the island so charming that the scene is set for an unforgettable holiday. Mauritius, a melting pot where past and present are smoothly blended together, offers an essential beauty that will compel to return to its shores time and time again.

DAY 02 – Mauritius – (North Island tour)

Breakfast will serve in hotel. Visit the capital of Mauritius where one could discover the fragrances and the spicy smell of the typical market of Port Louis Not far away from the market is Le Caudan waterfront, a place for the whole family. This shopping centre offers a wide range of activities such as movie theatres, casino, boutiques, craft markets, and a lot of restaurants offering all sorts of international cuisines. Dinner & Overnight at the Hotel

DAY 03 – Mauritius

Breakfast will serve in hotel. Day at Leisure. Dinner and overnight at the Hotel

DAY 04 – Mauritius – (IIe Aux Cerf’s)

Breakfast will serve in hotel. An hour drive to the east coast would bring you to the coast from where we would travel by boat to Ile Aux Cerfs. This island is only a twenty minute boat ride away from the main island and perhaps has the best beaches in Mauritius. The island is truly beautiful and a wide variety of water sports may be availed. Ile aux Cerfs has two restaurants and a professional golf course too. Dinner and overnight at the Hotel

DAY 05 - Mauritius–(South Island tour)

Breakfast will serve in hotel. Visit Warner Bros. Movie World on the Gold Coast, where movie magic happens every day! During the South Tour, you would be taken to the floral factory shop near the eth town of Curepipe. A visit to Le Trou Aux Cerfs, an extinct crater in the centre of the island is a must for its amazing views on neighboring towns and vast agricultural fields. Following this, we would visit the sacred lake of Grand Bassin also commonly referred to as the Ganga Talao. The largest fresh water reservoir of the island - Mare Aux Vacoas is also visited. Not far from Grand Bassin, is the Black River Gorges National Park view point where one would enjoy a breathtaking view of the gorges merging with the Indian Ocean. A winding road leads from Case Noyale village to the coloured earths of Chamarel: an undulating landscape of different and contrasting shades of colours. The different shades of blue, green, red and yellow are apparently the result of the erosion of the volcanic ash. The neighboring waterfalls of Chamarel rise from the moors and the native plant life. The site possesses a rare beauty.

DAY 06 - Mauritius - Departure

Breakfast will serve in hotel. Day at leisure on your own. Depart to airport to take your flight to Delhi
